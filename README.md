1. Organisation Overview 
Name: NKOSIYEPHANA DWEKU 
Brief history of the business organisation 
Nkosi’s construction was started and created by Nkosiyephana Dweku. Nkosi’s 
construction was created to build, to repair and demolish. It all started back in 2016, as 
a small business in town in Nelspruit. It aims to conquer the construction world and 
gain popularity in order to” build and repair everything broken”. 
Mission and vision statements 
Our mission is to build an empire big enough to compete with big companies in the 
game. 
Target audience 
We target large companies and businesses, people who need house repairs, people 
working with road repairs. We also target people who need demolition to be done. We 
are part of home builders, remodels and etc (tiff quillan,2025).

2. Website goals and Objectives 
Nkosi’s construction website plans on building trust with potential clients, making sure 
we provide what we were asked to deliver, make sure we are reliable and make sure we 
provide a service that matches the client’s satisfaction. Our main goal is to create a 
clear working website which will be easy to navigate through and clear to understand, 
making it easy for clients to be able to make a request for quotations and consultations.

Nkosi’s construction website should include a clean, user-friendly design with clear

Nkosi’s construction requires a solid technical foundation to work safely. This includes 
access to appropriate machinery and equipment with regular maintenance systems to 
ensure reliability. Skilled engineers and  project managers must be trained in modern 
construction methods, building codes, and safety standards. 
Timeline and Milestones 
Nkosi’s construction relies on a set timeline and clear milestones to make sure projects 
are completed efficiently and on time. The timeline typically outlines each phase of the 
project. The initial planning and design are put through site preparation and 
construction, to final inspection and handover, while allocating realistic timeframes for 
each stage. The main milestones, such as obtaining permits, completing the 
foundation, structural framing, and finishing work, serve as checkpoints to track 
progress and identify delays early. Effective monitoring of these milestones allows
